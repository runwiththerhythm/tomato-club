Why heritage tomatoes?

Heritage tomatoes often have deeper flavour, beautiful shapes and colours, and stories attached to how they were grown and passed down.

By growing and sharing them, we keep genetic diversity alive — a small but meaningful act against blandness in gardens and food systems.
