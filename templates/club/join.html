Join the Heritage Tomato Club

Ready to grow something more interesting than supermarket tomatoes? Joining the club gives you access to our Seed Library, seasonal seed offers by post, and a small, supportive community of UK growers.

Start with the Free Gardener tier for online resources, or choose a paid tier to receive heritage tomato seeds through the post each season.

1. Explore membership

Take a look at the membership tiers and choose the level that fits your growing plans and budget — from free digital access to seed-by-post and premium supporter options.

View membership options

2. Create your account

Sign up with your email so we can keep you updated and, later on, link your membership to your Seed Library activity and seed drops. All new members start with Free Gardener access.

Create an account

3. Start exploring seeds

Browse the Seed Library, note varieties you’d like to try, and keep an eye out for seasonal seed offers as the club grows. Paid tiers will receive seed drops by post during the main sowing season.

Browse Seed Library

Early days, honest expectations

The club is in its early “seedling” phase. Features like member dashboards, online payments, and automated seed allocations will be added gradually. For now, we’re focusing on getting the basics right: good varieties, clear communication, and a welcoming community.

Paid tiers, seed drops, and supporter perks are part of our roadmap. In the meantime, joining as a Free Gardener helps us grow the club and lets you be first to hear when new features go live.
