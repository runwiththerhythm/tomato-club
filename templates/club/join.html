Early days, honest expectations

The club is in its early “seedling” phase. Features like member dashboards, online payments, and automated seed allocations will be added gradually. For now, we’re focusing on getting the basics right: good varieties, clear communication, and a welcoming community.

If you’re happy to be part of that early shaping process — and don’t mind a few rough edges — you’re exactly the kind of member we’d love to have onboard.
